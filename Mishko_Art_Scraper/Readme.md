**1. Synopsis**
The Mishko Art Scraper is a tool designed to automate the extraction of art images from the Mishko website. By leveraging web scraping techniques, this tool efficiently scans through the website's pages and downloads all available artwork images for offline access, archiving, or further analysis. Whether you are a digital artist, art enthusiast, or researcher, the scraper helps you collect high-quality images of artwork, illustrations, and visual content in bulk, without manually downloading each image.
